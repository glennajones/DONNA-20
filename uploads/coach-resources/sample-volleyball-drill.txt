# Volleyball Coach Resources

## Training Drill: Spike Attack Formation

This document outlines the basic spike attack formation for volleyball teams.

### Setup:
1. Position setter at front right
2. Outside hitter at front left  
3. Middle blocker at front center
4. Back row players in defensive positions

### Execution:
1. Pass to setter
2. Setter delivers to attacker
3. Attacker spikes cross-court or down the line

### Key Points:
- Communication is essential
- Timing is critical
- Practice proper footwork
